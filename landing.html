Game Formats

Pick your format.
We'll keep the score.

Whether it's a full Ryder Cup-style tournament with teams and points, or a quick best ball before somebody's flight — Sandbagger has you covered.

🏆

Ryder Cup

Team vs team, match play format across multiple rounds. Trash talk officially encouraged.

Best Ball

Two partnerships go head-to-head. The lowest net score on each hole wins it for the team.

One player picks a partner each hole — or goes lone wolf. High risk, high reward, high chaos.

Win a hole and catch the rabbit. Lose it and someone else grabs it. Nobody's ever really sure who's winning.
